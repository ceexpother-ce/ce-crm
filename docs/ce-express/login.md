# Login Guide

## How to Log In to CE Express

### Step 1 — Open CE Express
Navigate to the CE Express URL in your browser (e.g. https://cecom2.cellular-expert.com/ce_express/).

### Step 2 — Login as ArcGIS
Click **Login as ArcGIS** and enter your ArcGIS Enterprise username and password.

### Step 3 — Open Map View
On success you are taken to the Network Data Management view. Click **Open Map View** to start working.

> **Note:** If you cannot log in, contact your ArcGIS Enterprise administrator to verify your account has CE Express access.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---|---|
| "Invalid credentials" | Check your ArcGIS Enterprise username/password |
| Page not loading | Check your network connection and the CE Express server URL |
| No Map View button | Your account may not have the correct role assigned |
